Case Study - Online Access

← Back

Mockup

When this project began, the first objective was the to bring the Online Access's website to match the new company branding (which you can read about here). The second was to clearly showcase the company's services, as well as have a touch of personalization. Modern and sleek was the initial design.

After a few passes on the initial mockup, this was the design settled upon. The team at the company is relatively small, so showing members of the team and the reviews was important, as well as leaving open several lines of communication for the customers.

Live Site

After the initial design had been finalized, the process of building the website was a whole other task. For one, there had to be the accomodation of the seasonal logos that were being designed, as well as finding a solution for image optimization, which at the time, webP was not supported on all major browsers.

The website was developed on Online Access's proprietary platform: PagePilot, and the web pages built with HTML. I handled building the HTML, CSS, and Javascript for the website, as well as producing the graphics for the homepage. It has seen plenty of content updates since the initial design, but the original heart is still there.
