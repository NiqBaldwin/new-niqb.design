About Niq

Hey there! I'm Niq (with a "Q"). I'm a rounded designer with a passion for creativity and technical skills. I currently serve as the team lead for the web design team at Online Access.

Born and raised in eastern Michigan, I grew up with a love for the arts, video games, and storytelling. Eventually I cultivated the skill to make it through college and earn my BFA, with an emphasis in Illustration.

I found myself in the world of web design not too long after that. Starting off as customer support, I eventually worked my way up into the design department, starting in UI/UX, eventually leading the web design team in the development department at Online Access. Since then, I've helped restructure the entire web development process, to a streamlined, modular workflow, and continue to work and improve.

Of course, design isn't my only skill, I've spent almost a decade using and growing my knowledge with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I've helped design and implement various UI, widgets, and more throughout the years. I also find a particular joy in learning new skills and being able to use said skills.

If you'd like to get to know me a bit more, or are looking for a rounded designer/developer, go ahead and reach out.
